Vijay

Vijay is a renowned Tamil actor, singer, and producer. Born on June 22, 1974, he is one of the most popular and influential actors in Tamil cinema.

Early Life

Vijay was born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. His father S. A. Chandrasekhar is a film director, and his mother Shoba is a playback singer. He studied at St. Bede's Anglo-Indian Higher Secondary School in Chennai.

Career

Vijay made his film debut in 1992 with the film "Naalaiya Theerpu". He gained widespread recognition with films like "Poove Unakkaga" (1996), "Padayottam" (1999), and "Ghilli" (2004). Some of his most successful films include:

  • Mersal (2017)
  • Bigil (2019)
  • Master (2021)
  • Beast (2022)
  • Varisu (2023)

Awards and Recognition

Vijay has received several awards and nominations for his performances, including multiple South Indian International Movie Awards (SIIMA) and Tamil Nadu State Film Awards. He is known for his dancing skills and energetic performances.

Personal Life

Vijay is married to Sangeetha Sornalingam and has two children. He is also involved in philanthropy and social causes. He is an ardent fan of Indian cinema and known for his humility and down-to-earth nature.
